What We Do ?

CraveYard™ is a food delivery website founded by Ayush Sharma. Its headquarters is located in New Delhi, India.

Features of Food Delivery Website

  • It is available in more than 600 cities spread across 56 countries
  • The website offers food delivery not just to homes and offices, but also to other locations like parks and grounds
  • People who want to make extra money can become dashers at CraveYard™ and deliver food
  • Project DASH is its CSR project, through which CraveYard™ donates excess food from restaurants to Feeding India Charity’s MealConnect system
